Zima Blue 6.5 in. x 8 in.
publication + video

A publication thats a typo-translation of the LOVE DEATH + ROBOTS animation–ZIMA BLUE. The book presents stills from the film as well as the full script. The book only uses blue as a color to allude to the character's obssession with a certain shade of blue. The film is staggered throughout the pages to highlight cinematic and profound moments in both the animation and the script. The publication is accompanied by a video reinterpetation of Zima’s fleeting words in the last minute of the film.
